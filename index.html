Alistair Cockburn

In 2015 Alistair launched the Heart of Agile movement which is presented as a response to the overly complex state of the Agile industry.



  • Life and career-Cockburn started studying the methods of object oriented (OO) software development for IBM. From 1994, he formed "Humans and Technology" in Salt Lake City. He obtained his degree in computer science at the Case Western Reserve University. In 2003 he received his PhD degree from the University of Oslo.
